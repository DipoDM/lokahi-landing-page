Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Lōkahi. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our mobile application and tell you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • Location data (with your permission)

Usage Data

  • Event interactions and bookings
  • App usage patterns
  • Device information

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Send you event notifications and updates
  • Process your event bookings and payments
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Communicate with you about our services

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability

Payment Processing and Financial Information

We use Stripe to process payments for event tickets and manage host payouts. When you make a purchase or receive payouts:

Payment Information

  • Credit/debit card details are collected and processed securely by Stripe
  • We do NOT store your full payment card numbers
  • Payment information is encrypted and handled according to PCI-DSS standards
  • Transaction data includes purchase history, amounts, dates, and refund status

Stripe Connect for Hosts

  • Event hosts who accept payments create Stripe Connect accounts
  • Host payout account information is managed by Stripe
  • We receive limited information about host account status and payout eligibility

Financial Data Retention

We retain financial data for legal and business purposes:

  • Transaction records: 7 years (tax and legal compliance requirements)
  • Refund requests: 2 years after resolution
  • Host payout history: Duration of active host account plus 7 years

For more information about how Stripe handles your data, see Stripe Privacy Policy and Stripe Services Agreement.

Tax Information

Hosts may be required to provide tax information (e.g., SSN, EIN) to Stripe for IRS reporting purposes. This information is collected and managed by Stripe, not Lōkahi.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics, authentication, and other app functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase (Backend/Database): Privacy Policy
  • Sentry (Error Tracking): Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In: Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign In: Privacy Policy
  • Stripe (Payment Processing): Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Support page.
